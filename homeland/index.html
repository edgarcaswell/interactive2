Larry Sultan | Homeland | 2006 - 2009
Homeland is a series of photographs that capture the haunting beauty of the migrant lives of hispanics in the Bay Area. With a solemn trust in the observer, Sultan gives you a look into the still beauty of life that goes unnoticed.

"What drives me to continue this work is difficult to name. It has more to do with love than with sociology. With being a subject in the drama rather than a witness. And in the odd and jumbled process of working, everything shifts: the boundaries blur, my distance slips, the arrogance and illusion of immunity falters.
